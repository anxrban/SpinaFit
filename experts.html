Quotes by experts

Client Profile

Dr. Krupa Patel PhD, McMaster University

"One interesting aspect of research into prevention of neural tube defects investigates the intake of folic acid by expecting mothers especially during the first trimester. It is interesting though because while intake of folic acid has been shown to reduce the occurrence of neural tube defects, the exact cause of spina bifida is yet to be determined." - Dr. Patel

Dr. Godfrey P. Oakley Jr. MD, MSPM

"Neural tube defects are not only a public health problem, they're a major cause of infant mortality, morbidity, and lifelong disability worldwide. Yet, these are largely preventable conditions. Folic acid fortification and supplementation have proven to be effective strategies in reducing the occurrence of these devastating birth defects." - Dr. Oakley Jr
